Rebecca (Yuxin) Bao

Undergraduate Researcher · UCLA Computer Science

I am a third-year Computer Science undergraduate at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), currently working as a research assistant at the VAIL Lab with Prof. Bolei Zhou and at the UCLA Mobility Lab with Prof. Jiaqi Ma.

My research focuses on end-to-end autonomous driving, simulation-to-real evaluation, and cooperative perception for intelligent transportation systems. I am especially interested in bridging the open-loop / closed-loop gap that limits how well learned driving policies transfer from training datasets to deployment-time environments.

Outside research, I work on robotics systems engineering (ROS1/2, LiDAR & camera pipelines), care a lot about clean production-ready code, and enjoy 3D printing, traditional Chinese painting, and classical guitar.
